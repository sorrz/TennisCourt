Starting Gear

We would like to remind you that there are some starting gear required for this course. You will need a pair of sneakers, preferably with good grip, but no duds on. Secondly you will need a tennis racket that fits your size. If you do not have a racket, you can rent one from us for the low price of 2$ per session. Showers, pool and sauna are open at all hours for course attendants.
